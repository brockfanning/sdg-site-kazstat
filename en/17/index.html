    Goal 17: Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the Global Partnership for Sustainable Development

    1. Home
    2. Goal 17

    Target 17.1
    17.1.1 Reported online Total government revenue as a proportion of GDP, by source
    17.1.2 Reported online Proportion of domestic budget funded by domestic taxes

    Target 17.2
    17.2.1 Reported online Net official development assistance, total and to least developed countries, as a proportion of the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD) Development Assistance Committee donors’ gross national income (GNI)

    Target 17.3
    17.3.1 Reported online Foreign direct investment (FDI), official development assistance and South-South cooperation as a proportion of total domestic budget
    17.3.1.1 Reported online Ratio of the gross outflow of direct investment abroad from Kazakh direct investors to GDP
    17.3.1.2 Reported online Ratio of the gross inflow of direct investment in Kazakhstan from foreign direct investors
    17.3.2 Reported online Volume of remittances (in United States dollars) as a proportion of total GDP

    Target 17.4
    17.4.1 Reported online The ratio of payments for the repayment and maintenance of external public and publicly guaranteed debt to the export of goods and services

    Target 17.5
    17.5.1 Exploring data sources Number of countries that adopt and implement investment promotion regimes for least developed countries

    Target 17.6
    17.6.1 Reported online Number of science and/or technology cooperation agreements and programmes between countries, by type of cooperation
    17.6.2 Reported online The number of fixed (wired) broadband Internet subscribers is broken down by speed

    Target 17.7
    17.7.1 Exploring data sources Total amount of approved funding for developing countries to promote the development, transfer, dissemination and diffusion of environmentally sound technologies

    Target 17.8
    17.8.1 Reported online Proportion of individuals using the Internet

    Target 17.9
    17.9.1 Reported online Technical and financial support provided by Kazakhstan to other countries, including within the framework of ODA, million US dollars

    Target 17.10
    17.10.1 Reported online The weighted average level of tariffs in Kazakhstan

    Target 17.11
    17.11.1 Reported online Proportion of developing countries and least developed countries in the import of Kazakhstan

    Target 17.12
    17.12.1 Reported online Average tariffs applied to developing countries and least developed countries

    Target 17.13
    17.13.1 Exploring data sources Macroeconomic Dashboard

    Target 17.14
    17.14.1 Reported online Number of countries with mechanisms in place to enhance policy coherence of sustainable development

    Target 17.15
    17.15.1 Exploring data sources Extent of use of country-owned results frameworks and planning tools by providers of development cooperation

    Target 17.16
    17.16.1 Exploring data sources Number of countries reporting progress in multi-stakeholder development effectiveness monitoring frameworks that support the achievement of the sustainable development goals

    Target 17.17
    17.17.1 Reported online Total amount of grants, government contracts to buy social services from Non-government Organizations (NGOs), based on service areas (in millions of tenge)

    Target 17.18
    17.18.1 Reported online Proportion of sustainable development indicators produced at the national level with full disaggregation when relevant to the target, in accordance with the Fundamental Principles of Official Statistics
    17.18.2 Exploring data sources Number of countries that have national statistical legislation that complies with the Fundamental Principles of Official Statistics
    17.18.3 Reported online Number of countries with a national statistical plan that is fully funded and under implementation, by source of funding

    Target 17.19
    17.19.1 Reported online Dollar value of all resources made available to strengthen statistical capacity in developing countries
    17.19.2 Reported online Proportion of countries that (a) have conducted at least one population and housing census in the last 10 years; and (b) have achieved 100 per cent birth registration and 80 per cent death registration
